About

I am a multidisciplinary artist / toolmaker / researcher who studied computer science and art at Carnegie Mellon University before working on bioinformatics at Rockefeller University. I am the founder / director of Morphogen, a generative design company developing Artbreeder, a massively collaborative creative tool and network. My works are somewhere in the region between art, design and research and inspired by the systems of biology computation and creativity.
